Google Drive Link Converter

Convert Google Drive share links to direct download links

How to use:

  1. Get a shareable link from Google Drive (right-click → Share → Copy link)
  2. Paste the link in the input box above
  3. Click "Convert" to get the direct download link
  4. Use the converted link in your websites or applications
